The Boy I Never Really Knew

2012 • Same Campus • Silent Admiration

There are some people who arrive in your life with a grand entrance, and there are some who quietly exist in the background for years before fate decides it is finally their turn. You were that person for me. Back in 2012, we walked the same campus grounds, breathed the same air, crossed the same pathways, and probably passed each other more times than either of us could ever count. We were close enough to know of each other, yet distant enough to remain strangers. We studied in different colleges within the same campus, lived completely separate lives, and never shared a real conversation. And yet, somewhere in those years, there was a silent awareness. We admired each other from afar without ever saying a word. Looking back now, it feels almost impossible that two people who would one day mean so much to each other could spend years being only familiar faces in the crowd. At the time, I never imagined that the boy I barely knew would one day become the person I would trust with my heart, my dreams, my fears, and my future. Life had already written the first page of our story. We just didn't know it yet.

A Message Sent Into The Future

October 22, 2019

On October 22, 2019, something ordinary happened. At least, it seemed ordinary at the time. A message appeared. A simple Facebook conversation. No dramatic beginning. No magical moment. No realization that this would become one of the most important conversations of my life. It was just two people talking. Looking back now, I smile because neither of us understood what that tiny moment truly was. It wasn't simply a message. It was fate quietly knocking on a door that would take years to fully open. We exchanged words, shared small pieces of our lives, and then continued living our separate journeys. If someone had shown me that conversation and told me, "One day this person will become your home," I would never have believed it. Yet today, every chapter that followed can be traced back to that simple message sent into the future.

The Friendship Written Between The Silences

2019 - 2025



The years that followed were not a straight line. They were filled with pauses, silences, disappearances, and unexpected returns. Sometimes we talked often. Sometimes months would pass without a single message. There were periods when life pulled us in completely different directions. We celebrated victories separately. We survived difficult seasons separately. Entire chapters of our lives unfolded without the other person being present. And yet somehow, no matter how much time passed, the conversation never truly ended. It only paused. Looking back now, I realize that what we had during those years was something rare. We never forced our friendship. We never demanded explanations. We simply found our way back to each other over and over again. The years between 2019 and 2025 became a collection of small conversations, random check-ins, and unfinished chapters. At the time they seemed insignificant. Today I see them differently. They were the threads quietly stitching together a story that neither of us fully understood.

The Roads We Had To Walk Alone

Life was not gentle with either of us. We each had roads we needed to walk alone. We made choices, experienced heartbreaks, learned painful lessons, and carried burdens that nobody else could fully understand. There were moments when happiness felt close and moments when it felt impossibly far away. There were dreams that worked out and dreams that fell apart. We grew through disappointment, responsibility, sacrifice, and change. Looking back now, I don't wish away any of those difficult years because they shaped the people we eventually became. The truth is that the version of me who met you again in 2026 was not the same woman who walked through those college corridors in 2012. And the man you became was not the same boy I once admired from afar. We both carried scars, wisdom, and experiences that only time could provide. Perhaps fate wasn't keeping us apart. Perhaps fate was preparing us.

The Little Girl Who Changed My World

Before our story became what it is today, another person completely transformed my world. Her name is Anvika. She brought meaning to my life in ways I never knew were possible. She taught me strength when I thought I had none left. She taught me patience, courage, and unconditional love. Every decision I make now carries her future within it. Every dream I have includes her. She is the most precious part of my life, and because of that, anyone who loves me must also love her. What makes me love you even more is not simply the way you treat me. It is the way you accept her as part of the story. You never saw her as an obstacle or a responsibility. You saw her as someone to protect, guide, and care for. And every time I imagine our future, one of the most beautiful thoughts I have is seeing the kind of father figure you could become in her life. I truly believe you will be the greatest dad she could ever ask for.

The Day The Stars Aligned

March 13, 2026

Then came March 13, 2026. Looking back now, it feels like the day the universe quietly changed direction. After years of friendship, years of almost, and years of timing that never quite worked, something shifted. The conversations became deeper. The walls became lower. The distance between two people who had known each other for years suddenly began to disappear. We talked about life, fears, dreams, failures, responsibilities, family, and the future. For the first time, it felt as though we were truly seeing each other. Not the versions we showed the world. Not the versions shaped by social media or casual conversations. The real versions. And somewhere in those conversations, without either of us planning it, friendship started becoming something more.

When Friendship Learned How To Love

May 11, 2026

On May 11, 2026, the words we had both carried quietly in our hearts finally found their way into the open. There was no dramatic movie scene. No fireworks. No audience. Just honesty. Pure, simple, beautiful honesty. After fourteen years of crossing paths, missing opportunities, growing separately, and finding our way back to each other, we finally admitted what had been waiting beneath the surface. It felt surreal. The person who had once been a distant face on a college campus was suddenly the person I wanted beside me every day. For years, life had given us almost. Almost conversations. Almost timing. Almost chances. But on that day, almost finally became us.

Home

For the first time in a very long time, I stopped wondering where I belonged. Home is often described as a place, but I have learned that sometimes home is a person. Home is the feeling of safety after a difficult day. Home is being understood without having to explain every thought. Home is knowing that someone chooses you even after seeing your flaws, your fears, and your past. When I think about you, that is the feeling I have. Not excitement alone. Not romance alone. Something deeper. Something calmer. A sense of peace. After everything life has taught me, that feeling is more valuable than anything else. And somehow, after fourteen years of unexpected twists and impossible timing, I found that feeling in you.

The Family We Haven't Met Yet

When I imagine the future now, I no longer imagine it alone. I imagine ordinary mornings filled with laughter. I imagine family dinners, school events, vacations, celebrations, challenges, and all the little moments that make up a life together. I imagine watching Anvika grow while knowing she is surrounded by love. I imagine building traditions, creating memories, and facing life's uncertainties side by side. The future will not always be easy. There will be challenges, disagreements, difficult seasons, and unexpected obstacles. But what matters is that we will face them together. The most beautiful part of our story is not what has already happened. It is everything that still hasn't happened yet. Our best chapters are still waiting to be written.

One Last Question

Abi, if someone had told me fourteen years ago that the boy I quietly admired from a distance would one day become the person I would trust with my entire heart, I would never have believed it. Life took us through separate journeys, separate heartbreaks, separate lessons, and separate battles before finally bringing us back together at exactly the right moment. Looking at our story now, I no longer believe in coincidence. I believe that some people are meant to find each other, even if it takes years. You know my past. You know my fears. You know my strengths and weaknesses. You know the parts of me that the world sees and the parts I usually keep hidden. And despite all of it, you stayed. You chose me. More importantly, you chose us. You chose a future that includes Anvika. You chose a life that comes with responsibilities, imperfections, and challenges. You chose love not because it was easy, but because it was real.

And so I have one final question.

Will you walk beside me through every victory and every setback?

Will you laugh with me during the happiest moments and hold my hand during the hardest ones?

Will you continue choosing me, choosing us, and choosing this family every day for the rest of our lives?

Because if you will...

Then I promise that no matter what the future brings, you will never have to walk alone again.
